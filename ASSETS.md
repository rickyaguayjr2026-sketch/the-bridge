# Third-party assets

Every non-code asset the app ships with (audio, images, fonts, etc.) is logged
here with its source and license, so the terms are on record rather than
relied on from memory. Add a new entry any time an asset is added.

## Video

### `app/src/main/res/raw/covenant_intro.mp4`
- **Content:** Covenant Intro cinematic sequence (John 1:1 -> paper/ember
  vortex into fire -> "Holy Holy Holy / Lord God Almighty / Worthy is the
  Lamb" with heart-and-rings motif), ~13s.
- **Generated with:** Grok Imagine (xAI), prompted and generated by Ricky.
- **Rights:** Per xAI's terms, the user owns Grok-generated outputs and may
  use them commercially; xAI provides no IP indemnification, so responsibility
  for any third-party rights issue in generated content rests with the user.
  No such issue is expected here (generic cinematic/text visuals from an
  original prompt).
- **Modification:** Original file included baked-in audio/music; that audio
  was stripped (`ffmpeg -an`) and replaced by muxing in
  `covenant_swell.mp3` (see below) as the single audio track. First version
  (2026-07-04) trimmed the audio to the video's ~10s length, which felt
  abrupt; fixed (2026-07-06) by holding the video's last frame for the
  remaining ~3s (`tpad`) instead, so the full ~13s track plays out over a
  frozen final frame rather than getting cut off.
- **Added:** 2026-07-04, audio-cutoff fix 2026-07-06

## Images

### `app/src/main/res/drawable/porch_door_scene.jpg`
- **Content:** Lit stone-archway doorway scene (lantern, warm wood door,
  candle visible through the open doorway) — used as the Home/Porch Intro
  focal scene, replacing an earlier geometric Canvas-drawn placeholder.
- **Generated with:** Grok Imagine (xAI), prompted and generated by Ricky
  (source file: `Stone_archway_with_golden_light_202607062151.jpeg`, the
  latest of several generated variants).
- **Rights:** Same basis as `covenant_intro.mp4` above — user owns
  Grok-generated outputs, no IP indemnification from xAI, no issue expected
  here (original prompt, generic scene).
- **Added:** 2026-07-06
- **Used in:** Home/Porch Intro screen

### `app/src/main/res/drawable/avatar_moses.jpg`
- **Content:** Moses portrait — full body, staff and stone tablets, on a
  clean white background. First of the 8 Biblical Avatars to get real
  artwork; the rest are still text-only cards pending backgrounds
  consistent with this one (the other generated candidates so far were on
  non-uniform dark vignette backgrounds, not usable without a proper
  cutout).
- **Generated with:** Grok Imagine (xAI), prompted and generated by Ricky
  (source file: `Moses_holding_staff_and_tablets_202607090113.jpeg`).
- **Rights:** Same basis as the other Grok Imagine assets above.
- **Added:** 2026-07-09
- **Used in:** Avatar Selection screen
